Privacy Policy for PsycomSpec AI Copilot

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

1. What We Collect

We collect the following data during your interaction:

  • Emotion selected (e.g., Frustrated, Anxious)
  • Text responses you provide (e.g., answers to “Why?” questions)
  • Timestamp of interaction
  • An anonymized session ID
  • Derived insights (e.g., “Heartbeat” and “Pulse” themes)

No names, contact details, or location data are collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely to:

  • Facilitate your reflection session
  • Log anonymous trends to identify common themes
  • Enable live visualizations (e.g., in Google Sheets dashboards)

3. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in a private Google Sheet via Zapier automation. Access is restricted to authorized administrators. Google Sheets and Zapier follow industry-standard security protocols.

4. User Rights

You may request deletion of any data tied to your session ID by contacting the administrator. As no identifying information is collected, deletion is based on timestamp and session ID only.

5. Consent

By using PsycomSpec, you consent to this data use and understand that all data is anonymous and used solely for reflection and insight purposes.
